Respan is committed to helping healthcare organizations maintain HIPAA compliance when using our platform.

## Business associate agreement (BAA)

For healthcare organizations that need to process Protected Health Information (PHI), Respan offers Business Associate Agreements.

<Card title="Request BAA" icon="file-contract" href="mailto:security@respan.ai?subject=BAA Request">
  Contact our security team to discuss your HIPAA compliance requirements and execute a BAA.
</Card>

## HIPAA-ready features


- **Data Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access control and audit logging
- **Data Residency**: Control over where your data is processed and stored
- **Audit Trails**: Comprehensive logging of all system activities
- **Secure Infrastructure**: SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure

## Implementation guidelines

### For healthcare organizations

1. **Execute BAA**: Contact us to establish the Business Associate Agreement
2. **Configure Settings**: Enable HIPAA-compliant configurations in your account
3. **Team Training**: Ensure your team understands HIPAA requirements
4. **Ongoing Monitoring**: Implement regular compliance reviews

### Technical safeguards

- Enable audit logging for all API calls
- Use encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- Implement proper access controls
- Regular security assessments
